    Introduction

    Min Yoongi is a Korean rapper, songwriter, and producer that works under two names, Suga and Agust D. When he is working under Suga, he is working as a member of the most popular boy band in the world, BTS. When he is working under Agust D, he is a solo artist. His music speaks for itself, so click on the tabs above to check out some of his amazing work. Click on the buttons below for different fun facts and quotes/lyrics!

    Fun Facts

    Yoongi chose to become a rapper after listening to the song 'Fly' by Epik High. He later went on to produce music for them.
